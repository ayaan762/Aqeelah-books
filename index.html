THE NAME

MY NAME IS ABBAS

BY THE GRACE OF GOD - INTRODUCING OUR FIRST RELEASE

We are honored to embark on this journey by telling the story of the son of Ali (a) and Ummul Baneen (a), the standard-bearer of Karbala and the moon of the Hashemi clan. The life of Abu al-Fadhl Abbas (a) is one of truth and sacrifice, of service to the Imam (a) of his time. Children will learn about the day of Ashura in a way that is meaningful and appropriate. This book is complete with vivid illustrations, discussion questions, and a glossary to support understanding. We hope this humble introduction to Abbas (a) inspires the younger generation to start building a legacy rooted in bravery, loyalty, and resilience.
