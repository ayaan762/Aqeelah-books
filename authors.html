The hearts behind the stories

We are honored to introduce the team behind Aqeelah Publishing - a husband & wife duo who embarked on a journey to write meaningful children's books rooted in the love of Allah and the teachings of the Ahl al-Bayt. Their adventure began in 2023 when the birth of their son inspired them to write about the very personalities they longed to teach him about.

They write with a shared vision: to nurture young hearts with wilayah and to craft books that families can turn to as a means of bonding, guidance, and love.
